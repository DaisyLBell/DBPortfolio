Moodboard

This project's conception started at an unconventional step: the moodboard. The mag's founders and I created a moodboard to act as a visual guide throughout the magazine production process. This preceded the style guide, color palette, and the logo.

Style Guide

I used the moodboard to create the style guide.

For so much of the style guide, the magazine needed a solid font type that would add visual interest without distracting from the zine's visual aesthetic: realism and collages. To achieve this result, I selected the sans-serif Forma DJR Banner by David Jonathan Ross. I also selected the mag's colors to ensure the its readability without digital eyestrain and a unique visual identity when printed.
