On-Boarding

Since this was a client-designer relationship, the biggest priority for me was to understand how I could help my client both with my visual skills and with my software expertise. I am the sort of designer who values function above all else, since assets serve a purpose to clients alongside the assets' visual appeal.

During this process, I wanted to make sure that I solved any potential obstacles after my work with the organization was complete. In this situation, I needed to create social media assets that were easily editable and assets that looked good next to other assets after being edited. In order to achieve these objectives, I created clearly labeled editable Photoshop documents using the software's Smart Object feature.

Brand Guidelines

In this project, I was hired to refresh the company's branding as well as their social media marketing. In order to achieve this task, I needed to know their target audience and how they planned to reach it. In other words, I needed to fully understand their branding priorities.

Since this brand showcases new musical talent, in which the musical talent has their own marketing aesthetic, I needed to develop a brand that wouldn't visually clash with a broad span of other graphics. In order to achieve this branding priority, I created a black and white color palette.
